📚 About This Series

This Physics Academy is designed specifically for 100-level Nigerian university students tackling PHY 105 (Mechanics and Properties of Matter). Each lesson uses past questions from institutions like UNILAG, UI, UNIBEN, and OAU to teach fundamental physics principles through active problem-solving. Where necessary, we also reinforce the underlying mathematics.
